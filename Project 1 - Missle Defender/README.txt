Project: Missile Defender
Author: Khoi Ho
Description: Missile Defender is a simple survival game made with Unity. In this game, the player controls a character and tries to shoot the enemies before they come close to the character.
Technology Used: Unity 2019.4.17f1 Personal
Features:
+ Point and click to shoot
+ Automatically spawned enemies
+ Minimap and vision cone
+ Particle effects
+ Score and best score
